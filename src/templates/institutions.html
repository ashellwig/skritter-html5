How it works

Contact us for trial or purchase a license
We offer a generous trial period free of change. You and your students will get free access and support with no purchasing commitment.
Choose a sign-up option

If your school purchases a license, we offer two options for students to create an account:

1. Get a batch of coupons that students can use to register on the site.

2. Have students sign up with a school email address.

Use your curriculum
Skritter already supports more than 150 textbooks, but if your institution uses a textbook we do not currently support, we will upload the first one for free with the purchase of school license.
